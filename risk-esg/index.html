How It Works

Three steps to your ESG Clearance

A transparent, structured audit pipeline from application to certified clearance.

1. Select Tier & Profile

Register your company and select your audit tier — from Gap Analysis to Full Certification. Complete the standard ESG screening questionnaire.

2. Upload & Pay

Submit your Environmental Impact Assessment and Governance Charter. Pay your tier's audit fee securely via Paystack to dispatch to the Ministry.

3. Receive Clearance

NEIIA specialists review your submission and forward to the Ministry of Environment. Once approved, your official Certificate is delivered to your dashboard.
