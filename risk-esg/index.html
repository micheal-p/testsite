How It Works

Three steps to your ESG Clearance

A transparent, structured audit pipeline from application to certified clearance.

1. Select Sector & Pay

Choose your business category — from Oil & Gas to Renewables — and pay the mandatory ₦300,000 audit fee securely via Paystack. Your application is locked in immediately.

2. Upload Documents

Submit your Environmental Impact Assessment, Social Policy, Governance Charter, and financial disclosures through our secure document portal for ECN review.

3. Receive Clearance

ECN auditors review your submission. Once approved, you receive an official Audited ESG Clearance Certificate delivered directly to your email.
