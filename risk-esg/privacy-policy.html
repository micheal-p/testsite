Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that identifies your corporate entity, including but not limited to corporate emails, facility locations, carbon emission logs, waste management policies, and financial identifiers necessary for processing audit fees.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data you submit through the ESG Clearance Portal is used exclusively for:

  • Assessing your company's compliance with Federal environmental standards.
  • Processing payments for audit reviews.
  • Generating and validating your official ESG Clearance Certificate.
  • Communicating assessment outcomes, required revisions, and clearance status.

3. Data Protection and Encryption

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your corporate data. Sensitive environmental audits and financial data are encrypted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology and securely routed through the Ministry's internal compliance servers.

4. Data Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable or corporate identifiable information. This does not include trusted independent auditors and Ministry officials who assist us in operating our clearance process, so long as those parties agree to keep this information strictly confidential.

5. Your Consent

By using our clearance portal, you consent to our web site privacy policy.
