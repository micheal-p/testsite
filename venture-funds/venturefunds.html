NEIIA supports a wide range of venture capital firms, from emerging managers launching their first funds to established institutions managing billions in AUM. Our platform is designed to handle the complexity of venture-specific fund structures with ease.
We provide institutional-grade fund administration, including comprehensive reporting, audit support, and tax preparation. Our tools ensure transparency and compliance, meeting the high standards required by institutional LPs.
Our software automates the most time-consuming aspects of fund management, from capital calls and distributions to investor onboarding. By consolidating your tech stack into a single dashboard, we eliminate manual errors and save your team hundreds of hours.
Unlike traditional administrators, NEIIA is a technology-first company. We combine deep venture expertise with a powerful software platform, allowing for faster closing times, cleaner data, and a superior experience for both GPs and LPs.
We offer a complete "fund-in-a-box" solution for emerging managers, covering entity formation, bank account opening, tax ID registration, and more. Our team guides you through every step of the launch process, so you can focus on raising capital.
