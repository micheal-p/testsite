FAQ

Common questions.

Still curious? Talk to the team.

Is this a chatbot?
Yes — but a useful one. Apex is conversational, connected to your Deal Room, and grounded in your real position data. Ask about your book, your pacing, your dispersion. It can also act in Deal Room when asked — share artifacts, send LP reports, advance a deal stage. No generic LLM hallucinations on top of nothing.
Where does the data live?
Inside your Deal Room tenancy. Apex doesn't move your positions out, doesn't train a shared model on them, and never blends them with another customer's data. Notes generate against your tenancy only.
What are the seven signals?
Exposure & concentration, vintage benchmarks (TVPI vs. peer cohort), pacing (called vs. committed), dispersion (best/worst spread), DPI vs. RVPI split, sector / geographic risk, and conviction (the recommendation). Each is computed per position; the note rolls them up to portfolio level.
Is the recommendation investment advice?
No. Apex notes are written as analyst commentary on your live data — useful for surfacing pacing risks, dispersion, and re-up cues — but they are not regulated investment advice. Always pair the output with your own diligence and your firm's compliance posture.
When does general availability land?
Targeted for Q3 2026, after the closed-beta cohort hits 25 firms and we have at least one full vintage of feedback. Until then, access is invitation-only via the waitlist.
