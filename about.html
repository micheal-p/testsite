About NEIIA

Powering Nigeria’s Energy Future Through Intelligence

The National Energy Investment & Intelligence Administration (NEIIA) serves as Nigeria's centralized platform for energy investment intelligence, asset visibility, and strategic coordination.
Explore NEFUND View National Grid

Bridging the gap between capital and sustainable energy development.

By aggregating data across funds, projects, SPVs, and energy assets, the platform enables government, investors, and stakeholders to make informed decisions, identify investment gaps, manage risk, and accelerate the implementation of national energy priorities.
