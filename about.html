About NEIIA

The energy intelligence layer for Nigeria.

The National Energy Investment & Intelligence Administration (NEIIA) consolidates capital, asset visibility, and strategic coordination for Nigeria's energy sector — bridging private capital and federal mandate under one operating system.

How we got here

Five decades, one architecture.

1977
NNPC established — consolidated petroleum operations under federal mandate.
1989
Energy Commission of Nigeria (ECN) chartered to coordinate national energy policy.
2005
EPSRA enacted — unbundles power sector, creates NERC.
2021
Petroleum Industry Act passes — NUPRC and NMDPRA replace DPR; NNPC becomes commercial entity.
2026
NEIIA established. One platform for capital, vehicles, assets and intelligence across the entire stack.
