FAQ

Your questions, answered.

Still wondering? Email the team.

What does it actually cost?
The platform itself is free. You pay per-company subscriptions to read full data on businesses you care about — typically $29–$79 per month, set by each publisher. Want to publish your own data? Free, but NEIIA collects 9.5% VAT on each sale. Want to be a Verified business so you can message verified enterprises? $40/month.
How does the publisher side work?
Open the Data Cell, add a publication for any company you own or represent, pick a monthly price, and start logging entries — risk notes, briefings, milestones, financials. Subscribers see updates the moment you publish. We collect payments and remit VAT; you keep 90.5% of every sale.
What does the 9.5% VAT cover?
The 9.5% is NEIIA's all-in platform fee, framed as VAT and remitted on your behalf. It covers payment processing, identity verification, hosting, support, the messaging infrastructure, and ongoing product development. There are no other charges — no setup fees, no annual minimums, no per-seat licences.
How do I become a verified business?
Settings → Plan & billing → Verified. Pay $40/month with the card on file. You get the gold ✓ badge across the platform, the ability to message any verified enterprise, an inbox toggle that restricts new threads to verified senders, and priority support. Cancel any time.
Can subscribers see my data without subscribing?
No. The Overview tab is a free preview — name, sector, headline metrics. The Financials, Deal Room and News tabs are behind a paywall on every company profile until the visitor subscribes. They see a clear paywall card with the price and a one-click subscribe button.
Do I have to publish anything to use Deal Room?
Not at all. Most users are subscribers, not publishers. Sign up free, browse companies, subscribe to the ones you want full data on, build a watchlist, message verified enterprises if you're verified yourself. The publishing tools are there if you ever want them.
