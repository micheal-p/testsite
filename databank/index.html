Key features

Essential infrastructure for modern fundraising

  • Clean and modern interface
  • Detailed tracking and analytics
  • Granular permissions and controls
  • Digital Subscriptions integration

Clean and modern interface

A crisp layout helps potential investors focus on the message and data that matters. Customize your data room experience with your firms logo, colors, and messaging to ensure potential investors remember your brand.

Detailed tracking and analytics

Track investor engagement and understand what material resonates with real-time notifications, document tracking, page-by-page analytics, and centralized reporting.

Granular permissions and controls

Create public or private share links while managing access at a room, folder, and file level, ensuring that data room visitors only see the information meant for them. Includes necessary compliance requirements such as watermarks, NDA pages, and audit trails.

Digital Subscriptions integration

Keep things simple for investors with direct integration to AngelList Digital Subscriptions. Investors can start a digital subscription straight from your Data Room.
