Common questions about Fin

Fin is an AI-powered private market intelligence tool built by NEIIA. Think of it like ChatGPT, but specifically designed for the investment ecosystem. You can research companies, analyze portfolios, benchmark valuations, track deal flow, and generate LP updates — all through a simple conversational interface powered by NEIIA's proprietary data network.

Fin is currently in private beta, available to firms on the NEIIA platform. You can join the waitlist and our team will reach out when a spot opens up.

Fin draws on NEIIA's proprietary data network, aggregating real-time information from thousands of funds, portfolio companies, and market transactions. This includes:

  • Funding rounds, valuations, and cap table data
  • Company financials, headcount, and growth metrics
  • Secondary market pricing and transaction data
  • LP and GP performance benchmarks
  • Sector and industry trend analysis

All data is sourced directly from NEIIA's platform ecosystem — not scraped or aggregated from third parties.

Unlike traditional market data providers, Fin is built natively on top of NEIIA's infrastructure. This gives you access to real-time, first-party data from thousands of funds — not scraped or aggregated secondhand data.
