Why Trade on NEIIA

Infrastructure you can trust

Purpose-built for energy commodity trading with institutional-grade security and regulatory oversight.

Secure Marketplace

Enterprise-grade encryption protects every transaction. All trades are verified and settled through secure, auditable channels with full traceability.

Standardized Contracts

Pre-built contract templates for electricity, gas, and carbon credit trades. Automated settlement mechanisms boost investor confidence and reduce disputes.

Regulatory Compliance

Full integration with Nigerian regulatory frameworks. NIN/BVN verification for individuals, CAC registration for organisations. Every trade is compliant by design.

Getting Started

How it works

From registration to your first trade in four simple steps.

1

Register

Sign up as an individual (NIN/BVN) or organisation (CAC). Choose your trading preferences.

2

Get Verified

Submit your documents for KYC/AML review. Admin approval ensures platform integrity.

3

List or Bid

List your energy assets for sale or place anonymous bids on existing listings. All bids are confidential.

4

Settle & Track

Trades settle via standardised contracts. Track your portfolio, profits and transaction history in real-time.
