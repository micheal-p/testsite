About

What is NEIIA?

The National Energy Investment & Intelligence Administration is the public-facing body coordinating capital formation for Nigeria's energy sector. The platform you're reading about is the operational layer — five connected modules that move energy assets, route equity, and serve LPs across the same record.

For investors

Start with the Deal Room guide if you want to subscribe to live deals, or the Energy Assets guide if you want to trade commodities. The LP Portal guide is most relevant once you're committed to a fund and need reporting.

For fund managers

Begin with NEFUND for the framework, then read the Deal Room guide for lifecycle mechanics. The LP Portal guide covers how to communicate calls, distributions, and quarterly reads to your investors. Apex AI sits on top of all of it.

For issuers

The Deal Room guide is the place to start — it covers KYC tiers, the listing workflow, lane routing, and what happens at close. Energy Assets is for offtake; everything equity-side lives in Deal Room.

Need a person?

Each guide ends with a contact card. For onboarding, KYC verification, or anything regulatory, the contact page routes you to the right desk. The Help center handles platform questions.
