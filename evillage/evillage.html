Anchored in the Smart Nation framework.

eVillage operationalizes the five strategic pillars of the Federal Government's Smart Nation agenda — translating presidential policy into measurable household, community, and institutional outcomes across Nigeria's energy sector.

Smart Governance

BVN/NIN-verified identity, NEMiC ledger transparency, and policy-aligned audit logs across every subsidy, transaction, and disbursement.

National Alignment · Renewed Hope Agenda

Built in lockstep with the Presidency, the Federal Ministry of Power, and the Energy Commission of Nigeria.

eVillage is the village-scale execution layer of the Renewed Hope Agenda — the policy mandate at the heart of President Bola Ahmed Tinubu's election commitment to the Federal Republic of Nigeria. Every disbursement, every kilowatt-hour delivered, and every subsidy claimed is accountable to a federal mandate and the PBAT Smart Nation framework.

THE PRESIDENCY

President Bola Ahmed Tinubu, GCFR

President & Commander-in-Chief, Federal Republic of Nigeria

The Renewed Hope Agenda's Smart Nation framework — Smart Governance, Economy, People, Infrastructure and Living — is the policy spine eVillage is built to operationalize at the village level. Mr. President's executive mandate sets the tempo for every household kilowatt-hour we deliver.

FEDERAL MINISTRY OF POWER

Chief Adebayo Adelabu

Honourable Minister of Power, Federal Republic of Nigeria

Policy oversight on grid expansion, the Presidential Metering Initiative, and the federal electrification mandate eVillage executes against — measured per household, per kilowatt-hour, per state. The Ministry's push for a Coordinating Minister for Energy further integrates this stack.

ENERGY COMMISSION OF NIGERIA

Dr. Mustapha Abdullahi

Director-General & CEO, Energy Commission of Nigeria

National energy strategy, planning, and inter-agency coordination — providing the regulatory and data backbone that lets eVillage operate at federal, sub-national, and community scale. Appointed by Mr. President in October 2023 to deliver clean-energy reforms through 2027 and beyond.

About eVillage

A federal energy access platform — engineered for citizens, built for the Renewed Hope mandate.

eVillage is the citizen-facing execution platform of the National Energy Investment & Intelligence Administration (NEIIA). It connects rural and peri-urban households to certified vendors, structured finance from NEFUND (the National Energy Fund), federal subsidies, and verifiable energy assets — under the policy oversight of the Federal Ministry of Power and the Energy Commission of Nigeria.

01

Solar Financing

Affordable payment plans for solar home systems with transparent loan tracking.

02

Mini-Grid Access

Connect to community mini-grids with pay-as-you-go metering solutions.

03

Subsidy Tracking

Real-time tracking of SHINE subsidies from application through disbursement.

04

NEMiC Transparency

Complete visibility into energy transactions and vendor payments.
