National Alignment · Renewed Hope Agenda

Built in lockstep with the Presidency, the Federal Ministry of Power, and the Energy Commission of Nigeria.

eVillage is the village-scale execution layer of the Renewed Hope Agenda — the policy mandate at the heart of President Bola Ahmed Tinubu's election commitment to the Federal Republic of Nigeria. Every disbursement, every kilowatt-hour delivered, and every subsidy claimed is accountable to a federal mandate and the PBAT Smart Nation framework.

Operationally, eVillage sits inside the federal energy-delivery architecture: planning is governed by the National Energy Masterplan Implementation Committee (NEMiC), capital flows through the National Energy Fund (NEFUND), and on-the-ground delivery is coordinated with the Federal Ministry of Power, the Rural Electrification Agency, and the Nigeria Governors' Forum so federal ambition translates directly into state and Local Government outcomes.

Every village node is registered against a federal household ID, every kilowatt-hour reconciled nightly with the Energy Commission of Nigeria reporting feeds, and every subsidy disbursement audit-trailed to the originating NEFUND tranche. The result is an execution system that the Presidency, the National Assembly, sub-national governments, multilateral lenders, and Nigerian citizens can each verify independently — the same source of truth, surfaced to each stakeholder through the role of dashboard they're accountable to.

About eVillage

A federal energy access platform — engineered for citizens, built for the Renewed Hope mandate.

eVillage is the citizen-facing execution platform of the National Energy Investment & Intelligence Administration (NEIIA). It connects rural and peri-urban households to certified vendors, structured finance from NEFUND (the National Energy Fund), federal subsidies, and verifiable energy assets — under the policy oversight of the Federal Ministry of Power and the Energy Commission of Nigeria.

01

Solar Financing

Affordable payment plans for solar home systems with transparent loan tracking.

02

Mini-Grid Access

Connect to community mini-grids with pay-as-you-go metering solutions.

03

Subsidy Tracking

Real-time tracking of SHINE subsidies from application through disbursement.

04

NEMiC Transparency

Complete visibility into energy transactions and vendor payments.
