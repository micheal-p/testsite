Key features

Modernize the fund subscription process

  • Investor passport
  • Custom onboarding workflows
  • Service provider collaboration
  • DocuSign integration

Investor passport

Make it easy for investors to reinvest by removing friction from the process. After the first commitment, AngelList creates a reusable profile that securely stores their essential information—enabling them to invest in minutes.

Custom onboarding workflows

Request only what’s needed from each LP, with real-time data validation to reduce errors. Onboarding flows are built in partnership with our team and customized to match your fund’s subscription documents.

Service provider collaboration

Set up dedicated dashboards with entity-level permissions for your fund admin & law firm partners to review LP submissions, conduct KYC/AML checks, and flag errors within a single platform.

DocuSign integration

Enable investors to sign finalized subscription documents effortlessly. Review and countersign everything from one centralized login.
