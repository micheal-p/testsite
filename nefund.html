Vehicle wrappers

Six wrappers, one wizard, inside Deal Room

SPV, Sub-Fund, Rolling, Venture, Co-Invest, Roll-Up. They used to be six product pages with overlapping marketing. They're now one dropdown in Step 2 of the new-deal wizard, with template defaults that pre-fill terms.

SPV
Single asset, single close. Flat ownership percentage. Defaults: 0% mgmt · 20% carry · min $25K.
Sub-Fund
Multi-asset, multiple closes. Commitment + called + unfunded + NAV across the basket. Defaults: 2/20 · min $50K.
Rolling Fund
Quarterly closes. Positions stacked by vintage. Each vintage carries its own NAV. Defaults: 2/20 · min $10K.
Venture Fund
10-year LP. Classic commitment + capital calls + distributions + NAV. Defaults: 2/20 · min $250K · 1–3 closes.
Co-Invest
Piggyback alongside a parent deal. Direct allocation, no fund-level fees. Defaults: 0% mgmt · 0% carry · min $50K.
Roll-Up
Consolidate prior positions into a single new vehicle at agreed conversion ratios. Defaults: 1% mgmt · 10% carry.

Investor lens

Investment Management, folded into Deal Room

No more separate IR product. The Portfolio page is the same Deal Room record with the camera flipped — every commit you make as an LP shows up as a position with KPIs, multiples, and an on-demand AI summary.

Live KPIs
Positions count, committed, called, distributed, NAV, TVPI, DPI. Per-position MOIC with status badges (green / info / warn).
AI summary
Reads exposure, pacing, dispersion, vintage benchmarks. Writes a written analyst-style note with a recommendation in seconds.
Soft → Hard → Signed
Every commitment carries a status. GPs see the live roster on the Outreach tab; LPs see the same position in their portfolio.
Real documents
File uploads per stage — deal letter, PPM, sub-doc, capital call notice, quarterly LP update. Aggregated in Reports.
USD / ₦ Naira toggle
Segmented switch in the workspace header flips every dollar figure to Naira on the fly. Storage stays USD-canonical.
Make a deal from any company
Every company row in the marketplace has a + Deal button. Opens the wizard pre-filled with that company as the target.
