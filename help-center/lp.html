Using the LP Portal

The LP Portal is your secure gateway to monitor your investments, review capital calls, and access confidential reports shared by General Partners through the Deal Room. Designed specifically for institutional investors and family offices, it provides a unified, consolidated view of your energy asset exposure across multiple managers and funds.

Instead of managing dozens of disparate data rooms and email threads, the LP Portal aggregates your positions into a single, standardized dashboard with real-time performance metrics.

Viewing Your Commitments

Once you are granted access to a fund and your subscription is finalized, the vehicle will appear in your LP Dashboard. You can track your total commitment, called capital, and outstanding capital across all your active investments in the Energy sector.

  • Performance Metrics: Instantly view your TVPI (Total Value to Paid-In), DPI (Distributions to Paid-In), and Net IRR across your portfolio, updated dynamically as GPs log distributions.
  • Asset Transparency: Drill down into specific funds to view the underlying portfolio companies and physical energy assets (such as solar farms or grid infrastructure) driving the returns.
  • Consolidated Reporting: Export your entire portfolio's performance into a single, boardroom-ready PDF or Excel file with one click.

Handling Capital Calls

Capital calls are handled entirely within the portal, ensuring security and an unalterable audit trail.

  • Notifications & Delivery: You will receive a cryptographic email and a platform alert when a new capital call is issued. The official notice is always hosted securely on NEIIA.
  • Verified Wiring Instructions: Wire details are securely provided within the call notice and are locked against tampering. Always verify wire instructions via the portal rather than email to avoid phishing or wire fraud.
  • Funding Status: Mark a call as "Wire Initiated" once you send funds. The GP will subsequently confirm receipt on their end, automatically updating your funded percentage and capital account balance.

Troubleshooting

I was invited to a fund but I don't see it in my dashboard.

Ensure that you have accepted the invitation using the exact email address it was sent to. If you have multiple NEIIA accounts (e.g., a personal and a corporate account), log into the one associated with the invitation link. If the issue persists, the GP may not have finalized your countersignature.

How do I download K-1s and tax documents?

Tax documents are securely uploaded to the "Vault" section of your specific fund view. Navigate to the fund, select "Vault", and look for the "Tax Documents" folder. You can download these in bulk.

Can I delegate access to my accounting team?

Yes. Go to "Account Settings" -> "Delegates" to invite your CPAs or legal team. You can assign them "Read-Only" or "Tax & Reporting Only" access so they can pull K-1s without seeing your capital call notices.
