Using the LP Portal

The LP Portal is your secure gateway to monitor your investments, review capital calls, and access confidential reports shared by General Partners through the Deal Room.

Viewing Your Commitments

Once you are granted access to a fund, it will appear in your LP Dashboard. You can track your total commitment, called capital, and outstanding capital across all your active investments in the Energy sector.

Handling Capital Calls

  • Notifications: You will receive an email and a platform alert when a new capital call is issued.
  • Wiring Instructions: Verified wire details are securely provided within the call notice. Always verify via the platform rather than email to avoid phishing.
  • Status: Mark a call as "Funded" once the wire is initiated. The GP will confirm receipt on their end, updating your funded percentage.

Troubleshooting

I was invited to a fund but I don't see it in my dashboard.

Ensure that you have accepted the invitation using the exact email address it was sent to. If you have multiple NEIIA accounts, log into the one associated with the invitation link.

How do I download K-1s and tax documents?

Tax documents are securely uploaded to the "Vault" section of your specific fund view. Navigate to the fund, select "Vault", and look for the "Tax Documents" folder.
