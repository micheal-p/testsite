Managing Risk & ESG

The Risk & ESG module allows asset managers and operators to track, report, and improve their Environmental, Social, and Governance metrics in compliance with global energy standards.

Carbon Tracking

Connect your operational data feeds or upload monthly reports to automatically calculate Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions. The platform uses localized grid emissions factors to ensure accuracy.

Regulatory Compliance

  • Frameworks: We support reporting aligned with TCFD, SASB, and the latest ISSB standards.
  • Automated Reports: Generate quarterly compliance reports with one click, ready for distribution to LPs via the Deal Room.
  • Benchmarking: Compare your portfolio's carbon intensity against industry averages.

Troubleshooting

How do I integrate a new smart meter API?

Navigate to Settings > Integrations in the ESG module. Select "Add Data Source", choose your vendor, and provide the read-only API key.

Can LPs see raw ESG data?

No, by default LPs only see the aggregated metrics published in the Deal Room. You can choose to permission raw data to specific LPs if requested.
